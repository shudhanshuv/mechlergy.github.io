About Us

Our company is a manufacturing-based company which is located in Bangalore, India. It was founded in Oct, 2021 with an aim to develop and innovate new technologies in the existing infrastructure surrounding us. As more and more new technologies coming into the market the world is moving towards greener path. But it is a very difficult task to overcome the existing technologies that are operating around us. In Mechlergy Pvt. Ltd., we provide idea and solutions to make the existing technology cleaner and greener. Our vision is to “generate clean and green energy from the mechanical world and make a better place to live in coming future”.
